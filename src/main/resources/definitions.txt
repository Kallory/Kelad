 from smallest to biggest -

Tiles (previously partofRoom) - smallest unit
Cells (previously Rooms) - has multiple tiles
Clusters (previously clusters) - has multiple cells
subRegions (no change, they are essentially neighborhood sized objects) - has multiple clusters
Regions (previously megaRegion) - multiple subregions
Zones (previously cities) - multiple regions
MegaRegion (Collection of zones) - collection of zones - mostly used for weather/culture
Divisions (previously province) - province/state level size collection of Zones/megaregions
 Domains (previously countries) - collection of divisions
